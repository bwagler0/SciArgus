#Landscape Conservation
Research on landscape conservation for any taxa (including birds, mammals, plants). Topics shoudl include habitat connectivity, wildlife corridors, mitigating fragmentation, climate change resilience, sustainable land use, conservation easments, and regenerative agriculture. I welcome thought leadership, perspectives, new tools, novel research, and management or conservation implications. 

#Species Distribution Models
Any research using species distribution models. Including methods, new applications, and best practices.

#Nutritional Ecology
Research on nutritional ecology. Topics should include all taxa with a focus on life history strategies, annual cycle ecology, maternal effects, interactions between nutrition and disease, and more. 

#Movement Ecology
Research on new advances in movement ecology of wildlife (including birds and mammals). Topics should include new methods, best practices, and new advacements in our understanding of wildlife movement.

#Wildlife and Natural Resource Management
Papers that inform wildlife and natrural resource management including research that informs or evaulates management decisions or actiosn such as hunting seasons, seasonal closures, protection of seasonal ranges, selective culling, habitat restoration, habitat imporovment projects, controled burns, grazing regimes, sustainable ranching practices, conservation easments, or others. Topics shoudl include evaluation of management and conservation actions or research with strong implications. 

#Natural Resource Extraction
Research that informs the effects of natural resource extraction (e.g. critical minteral mining, renewable energy, fossil fuels) on wildlife or landscapes. Can inlcude papers that inform policy decisions and those that are focused on the effects of habitat alteration.
